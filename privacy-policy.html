Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how FxCalc Fever with beauty collects, uses, and shares information when you use this mobile application.

1. Information I Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

I do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use FxCalc Fever with beauty. However, I may collect:

  • Currency preferences and settings stored locally on your device
  • In-app purchase transaction data (processed by Apple/Google)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the app, I may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction data, crash reports
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by advertising partners (see Section 3)

2. How I Use Your Information

I use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving the currency exchange calculation services
  • Displaying personalized advertisements
  • Analyzing app usage and performance
  • Processing in-app purchases
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

I use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use information about you to provide personalized ads. This includes:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • IP address and geographic location
  • Device information and app usage data

For more information about how Google uses your data, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

3.2 Apple App Store / Google Play Store

In-app purchases are processed by Apple (for iOS) or Google (for Android). I do not store your payment information. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Your Privacy Choices

4.1 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Object: Object to data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (see Section 4.3)

4.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

4.3 Advertising Choices

You can control personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (disable)
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • In-App: When prompted by the consent dialog, you can choose to limit ad personalization
  • Remove Ads: Purchase the "Remove Banner Ads" in-app product to remove banner advertisements (rewarded ads remain available for flag recovery)

5. Data Retention

I retain data only as long as necessary to provide the services and comply with legal obligations. Most data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 years of age. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe I have collected information from a child under 13, please contact me.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. I ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

8. Security
